About Us

We’re more than just a small business—we’re a tight-knit team of engineers, integrators, and cybersecurity experts dedicated to protecting what matters most. Our work happens behind the scenes, focused on critical missions where data and system integrity are non-negotiable. We specialize in taking complex, disparate systems and making them work together—securely, efficiently, and at scale. Whether it's high-speed system integration or building next-gen cyber defenses from the ground up, we thrive on delivering verifiable security and finding the truth in data. Veracity Cyber was formed to do things differently: to foster a culture of deep technical expertise, trust, transparency, with a genuine commitment to safeguarding our world every day.

Our Expertise

🧠 DataOps

We don’t just move data around. We transform, enrich, normalize, and make data usable. We’re experts with tools like NiFi, Splunk, Elasticsearch, and Cribl.

🛡 Data Analytics & Governance

At Veracity, we “find the truth in data” by unifying sources, automating governance, and delivering analytics you can act on. From lineage and catalogs to policy enforcement and secure sharing, we make data reliable, compliant, and ready for mission decisions.

🌐 Cloud & DevOps

Our Cloud & DevOps engineers build scalable, secure solutions in AWS—containerizing services with Docker, orchestrating them on Kubernetes, and automating configuration and deployments with Ansible.

Full Spectrum Cyber

From operating systems to embedded devices, we design, write, and audit low-level code and build custom defensive tooling—delivering verifiable security where it matters most, closest to the metal.
